The Revolution in Virtual Reality

Features

Easy to connect

Instead of viewing a screen in front of them, users are immersed and able to interact with 3D worlds. This creates a sense of presence, making the experience feel more lifelike and engaging.

VR Capability

Virtual reality (VR) can create an environment similar to or completely different from the real world. It enables users to explore and interact with 3D spaces that mimic or defy reality.

Video games

Ability to recognize hand motion, evolving interaction and gameplay in VR, allowing users to perform natural gestures for more immersive and intuitive experiences.

Interaction

Interaction refers to the natural interaction between the users and the virtual scene. It enables users to engage with the environment through gestures, voice commands, controllers, or eye movements.

Easy to play

In 360-degree video, the locations of viewers are fixed, viewers are limited to the angles captured by the cameras Unlike VR, it does not allow users to move freely within the environment but only to look around from a single point .

Awesome Experience

Virtual reality (VR) is a simulated experience that can be similar to or completely different from the real world. It immerses users in a computer-generated environment, often through headsets and motion-tracking devices.
